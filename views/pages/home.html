JavaScript sucks... but not for the reasons you might think

JavaScript is probably one of the most hated languages in history. There are countless articles about why it sucks.

However, most of the time, they tend to bash JavaScript on OO principle it does not have. Why? Why would JavaScript need any kind of OO content? This article is a listing of what JavaScript is really missing.
